EDUCATION THROUGH FOOTBALL

Our curriculum is designed around the four fundamental aspects of football: Technique, Tactics, Physical Abilities, and Mental Abilities.

These pillars serve as the foundation for children's holistic development and improvement in soccer skills. Moreover, they enable young players to approach situations from unique perspectives and seek alternative solutions.

Football is a demanding sport that provides players with opportunities to enhance their speed, agility, strength, hand-eye coordination, and cardiovascular endurance. Recognizing that the game offers more than just the excitement of competition, we acknowledge that playing football contributes to the development of essential social skills, the formation of new friendships, and the cultivation of self-confidence and self-esteem. Additionally, it teaches values such as respect, teamwork, loyalty, discipline, and the importance of hard work.

Furthermore, we understand that physically fit children are often associated with better academic performance, suggesting a positive correlation between physical fitness and educational achievements.

Our Mission

OUR MISSION, OUR VISION

The Football Academy was formed to provide the opportunities for youth elite soccer players to train and compete in Dubai and internationally.

Our mission is to develop soccer skills, as well as maintain an environment that embodies the core values of integrity, leadership, perseverance, respect and teamwork while enhancing self-esteem, promoting self-confidence and cultivating a life long passion for the beautiful game of soccer.

While our teams strive to compete at the highest levels of competition, TFA is fully committed to player development.

We believe that a commitment to player development establishes a solid technical and mental foundation for which to continue building upon at TFA and beyond.

We intend to accomplish the mission with experienced coaching staff that represent the most effective and comprehensive club soccer program in Dubai.
